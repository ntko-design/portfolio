    I like making pretty interfaces to help people work better.

    From doodling and making paper crafts, to designing high school yearbook spreads and pursuing a formal degree in design, I always felt that I was meant to do something in the creative field—and this passion led me to a career in product and UX. Though my understanding of the industry only continues to grow, I know one thing for sure: creating seamless and engaging experiences that captivate people and streamline how they approach their craft is what I strive to accomplish in each and every opportunity I come across.

    Having spent the better part of 3 years working in enterprise and B2B startup environments, I come from a background of designing and researching for SaaS products, and have extensive experience interfacing with enterprise clients and users. In particular, I helped create and refine experiences for an enterprise security and management platform, competitive intelligence platform, and a texting web application for medical professionals.

    Outside of work, my free time is split between various hobbies:

    • 🎮 Video games
    • 📺 Shows and movies
    • 🤖 Building model kits
    • 🍛 Trying my hand at new recipes
    • 🏃 Running for my life to set new PRs
    • 📚 Reorganizing my bookshelf for the umpteenth time
    • ✈️ And I love traveling, of course. But who doesn't in this day and age?
